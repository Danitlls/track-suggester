Find out your best match in the tech world

No one can deny that careers in information technology are here to stay. The field is attractive, lucrative, and supporting a booming industry. This quiz is designed to help you decide which major branch of the technology industry you're most likely to enjoy. While it is no replacement for a job counselor, this quiz is based on industry statistics and research and should help to point you in the direction that you may find interesting.

This test has three types of questions: Self-report, scenarios, and skill-testing questions. For the self-report and scenario questions, choose responses that best apply to you or that best describe how you would respond to a situation. Select answers that are based on your typical thoughts, feelings, and behavior. For the skill-testing questions, choose what you believe is the correct answer.

After finishing the test, you will receive a Snapshot Report with an introduction, a graph and a personalized interpretation for one of your test scores. You will then have the option to purchase the full results.
